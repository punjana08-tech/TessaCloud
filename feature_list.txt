1. Understanding of Client Requirements
---------------------------------------
The client requires a software system to track company-owned assets
and identify which employee is currently responsible for each asset.
The goal is to maintain accountability and easy visibility of assets.


2. Creative Freedoms Taken
--------------------------
- Product named "AssetTrack"
- Assumed a web-based internal system
- Introduced admin and employee roles
- Expanded scope to include asset history and status tracking


3. Assumptions Made
-------------------
- Each asset has a unique asset ID
- Each employee has a unique employee ID
- One asset can be assigned to only one employee at a time
- Assets can be reassigned
- Admin manages assets and assignments
- Employees can view their assigned assets


4. Feature List
---------------
1. Asset Registration
   - Add and manage company assets.

2. Employee Management
   - Add and maintain employee details.

3. Asset Assignment
   - Assign assets to employees.

4. Asset Reassignment
   - Transfer assets between employees.

5. Asset Status Tracking
   - Track asset availability and condition.

6. Asset History
   - View past assignments of assets.
